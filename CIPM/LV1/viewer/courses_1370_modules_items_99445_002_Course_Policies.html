Module 0: Getting Started 0.02 | Course Policies

Learner Etiquette  

Please review the following communication guidelines. Failure to comply with these guidelines may result in disciplinary action.  

  • Always be respectful, courteous, and considerate in your communications 
  • Do not use offensive language, personal attacks, or any form of discrimination 
  • Abide by the Code of Conduct 
  • Be respectful of privacy and mindful of what personal information you are sharing with your community 

If you have a concern about something shared in a discussion forum or elsewhere in the course, please message Program Staff from the Inbox in the navigation bar. If your concern is with a discussion post, you can click the three dots on the right side and then click ‘Report’. Select the reason for reporting the post and then ‘Submit’. Program Staff will be notified. Note that once you report a post, it cannot be undone.   

Code of Conduct 

In addition to abiding by the pledge you agreed to at registration, you are required to adhere to the policies governing the CIPM Program, as outlined by CFA Institute. These policies ensure the integrity, security, and fairness of the exam process. Any misconduct—defined as any activity or behavior that compromises or attempts to compromise the reputation, validity, or security of the CIPM Program—is strictly prohibited.

By participating in the CIPM Program, you agree to uphold the following principles of academic integrity:

  • You will abide by CFA Institute policies and complete all required elements appropriately to earn your certification.
  • You affirm that you will not give or receive any unauthorized assistance on required course activities, assignments, or exams. All submitted work must be your own.
  • You acknowledge that CFA Institute retains records of candidate activity within its platform. While you may retain downloadable materials and templates for personal use in your professional role, you are not permitted to distribute or share downloaded content with third parties.

Failure to comply with these policies may result in disciplinary action, including the withholding or revocation of your CIPM exam results or certification status. For a full list of CIPM Program policies, please refer to the CFA Institute Governance and Policies page.
